## Overview

The 28-Day program is our most comprehensive offering - a full lunar cycle dedicated to transformation. This duration allows for the complete arc: deep settling, thorough preparation, meaningful ceremony, extended integration, and the beginning of embodiment.

**Duration**: 28 days / 27 nights
**Ceremonies**: 1-2 (based on assessment)
**Post-Care**: 12 months standard

One month provides:

This program is for those ready for comprehensive transformation and able to dedicate a full month to their healing.
