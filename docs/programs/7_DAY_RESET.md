# 7-Day Nervous System Reset

> *A focused journey for reset and renewal*

---

## Overview

The 7-Day Reset is our most accessible program, designed for those seeking a powerful introduction to the Transformational Epicenter experience, professionals with limited time, or alumni returning for maintenance.

**Duration**: 7 days / 6 nights
**Ceremonies**: 1
**Post-Care**: 3 months standard

---

## Who This Program Serves

### Ideal Candidates

- First-time explorers wanting to experience our approach
- Busy professionals who cannot take extended leave
- Alumni returning for nervous system maintenance
- Those with simpler, more focused healing goals
- People with strong existing support systems
- Those who have done significant prior work

### May Not Be Right For

- Complex trauma requiring extended processing
- Those without existing therapeutic support
- First-time medicine experience with complex history
- Those seeking major life restructuring
- People needing extensive preparation support

## Day-by-Day Journey

### Day 1: Arrival & Welcome

**Morning/Afternoon**
- Airport pickup and transfer
- Welcome and orientation
- Room settling
- Light refreshments

**Late Afternoon**
- Medical assessment
- Final labs if needed
- Vital signs baseline
- Medication review

**Evening**
- Welcome dinner
- Meet staff and any fellow guests
- Orientation to space
- Rest and settle

---

### Day 2: Preparation

**Morning**
- Morning breathwork session
- Breakfast
- Medical clearance finalization
- Intention setting session

**Midday**
- Lunch
- Bio-optimization introduction
- Sauna + cold plunge session

**Afternoon**
- Therapeutic massage
- Rest time
- Nature walk

**Evening**
- Light dinner
- Preparation practices
- Early rest

---

### Day 3: Deep Preparation

**Morning**
- Morning practice
- Breakfast
- Integration session (pre-ceremony)
- Deeper intention work

**Midday**
- Light lunch
- Bio-optimization session
- HBOT or red light

**Afternoon**
- Massage
- Final preparation
- Quiet time

**Evening**
- Light early dinner
- Ceremony briefing
- Preparation practices
- Early rest

---

### Day 4: Ceremony Day

**Morning**
- Light activity or rest
- Final medical check
- IV access established
- Monitoring connected
- Space preparation

**Ceremony Window**
- Ceremony conducted per protocol
- Medical monitoring continuous
- Facilitator present
- Support as needed

**Through Night**
- Continuous care
- Rest as possible
- Medical observation
- Support available

---

- Rest continues
- Light nourishment when ready

- Gentle care

**Midday**

**Afternoon**
- First integration session
- Journaling time
- Nature if feeling ready

**Evening**
- Dinner
- Rest
- Optional gentle community

**Morning**
- Morning practice (gentle)

**Afternoon**
- Massage (recovery focus)

### Day 7: Departure

**Morning**
- Morning practice
- Final integration session
