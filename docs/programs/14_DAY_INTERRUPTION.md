# 14-Day Trauma Interruption & Integration

> *Deep work with space to process*

---

## Overview

The 14-Day program is our most popular offering, providing the optimal balance between depth and practicality. Two weeks allows for proper preparation, meaningful ceremony, and sufficient integration time before returning to life.

**Duration**: 14 days / 13 nights
**Ceremonies**: 1-2 (based on assessment)
**Post-Care**: 6 months standard

---

## Who This Program Serves

### Ideal Candidates

- Those seeking significant transformation
- Trauma processing with integration time
- Life transition support
- Career or purpose reevaluation
- Relationship pattern work
- Those able to take two weeks away
- First-time guests with moderate complexity

### May Not Be Right For

- Very complex trauma histories (consider 21-28)
- Those needing minimal intervention (consider 7)
- Those unable to take full two weeks
- Those needing extended preparation support

## Program Phases

### Phase 1: Arrival & Settling (Days 1-2)

**Purpose**: Transition from ordinary life, settle nervous system, complete assessments

**Day 1**
- Morning/afternoon arrival
- Welcome and orientation
- Room settling
- Medical assessment
- Welcome dinner
- Rest

**Day 2**
- Morning practice
- Facility orientation
- Integration session (baseline)
- Bio-optimization introduction
- Therapeutic massage
- Community dinner

---

### Phase 2: Deep Preparation (Days 3-5)

**Purpose**: Prepare body, mind, and spirit for ceremony

**Day 3**
- Morning practice
- Intention setting session
- Bio-optimization (sauna + cold)
- Massage
- Preparation content
- Evening practice

**Day 4**
- Morning practice
- Somatic preparation session
- HBOT session
- Therapeutic massage
- Nature immersion
- Early dinner and rest

**Day 5**
- Gentle morning
- Final preparation session
- Light bio-optimization
- Ceremony briefing
- Light early dinner
- Preparation practices
- Early rest

---

### Phase 3: Ceremony (Day 6)

**Purpose**: The journey

**Day 6**
- Morning: Final medical check, IV access, monitoring setup
- Ceremony: Full ceremony with continuous monitoring
- Through night: Care and observation
- Facilitator and medical presence throughout

---

### Phase 4: Primary Integration (Days 7-10)

**Purpose**: Process the experience, begin making meaning

**Day 7 (Post-Ceremony Day 1)**
- Rest and recovery
- Light nourishment when ready
- Medical monitoring continues
- Gentle support available
- First integration session (brief)

**Day 8**
- More active recovery
- Integration session
- Gentle movement
- Nature time
- Journaling
- Community gathering (optional)

**Day 9**
- Integration session
- Bio-optimization (recovery focus)
- Massage
- Deeper processing
- Art/expression time

**Day 10**
- Integration session
- Bio-optimization
- Massage
- Group integration circle
- Evening reflection

---

### Phase 5: Deeper Integration (Days 11-13)

**Purpose**: Connect insights to life, begin transformation anchoring

**Day 11**
- Morning practice
- Life integration session
- Bio-optimization
- Massage
- Values and priorities work
- Community time

**Day 12**
- Integration session
- Body-based integration
- Bio-optimization
- Nature immersion
- Life planning work
- Farewell dinner

**Day 13**
- Morning practice
- Final integration session
- Post-care protocol review
- App introduction and setup
- Departure preparation
- Closing ritual

---

### Day 14: Departure

- Morning practice
- Final reflections
- Checkout
- Farewells
- Transportation to airport

## Ceremony Decision

### One Ceremony Path
- Most common
- Full dose experience
- Extended integration time
- Recommended for first-timers

### Two Ceremony Path (When Appropriate)
- Initial ceremony + follow-up
- Greater depth potential
- Requires medical clearance
- More demanding on body
- Extended monitoring

**Decision factors**:
- Guest goals and history
- Response to first ceremony
- Medical considerations
- Time for integration
- Physician recommendation
