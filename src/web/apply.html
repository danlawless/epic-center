Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Our facility operates in Mexico where ibogaine treatment is legal and not a controlled substance. We maintain full compliance with all local medical regulations and licensing requirements. Our medical team adheres to rigorous safety protocols that often exceed international standards.

Our programs are designed for adults who are ready for deep transformation. Common reasons guests come to us include: trauma processing, addiction recovery (particularly opioids), treatment-resistant depression and anxiety, PTSD, existential exploration, and major life transitions. Medical screening determines eligibility - not all applicants are accepted due to safety considerations.

All guests undergo comprehensive medical screening including cardiovascular assessment (ECG required), laboratory testing, psychiatric evaluation, and medication review. Certain conditions are absolute contraindications - our Science page details these. Safety is our sacred responsibility, and we cannot accept guests who present significant medical risk.

Programs range from 7 to 28 days. We recommend adding 1-2 days on either end for travel. Most importantly, plan for a gentle re-entry period when you return home - avoid scheduling major obligations for at least a week after your program ends. Your nervous system will be in an open, sensitive state that benefits from continued rest.

All programs are fully inclusive: private luxury accommodation, all meals, daily massage, full bio-optimization access, ceremony/ceremonies, integration sessions, 24/7 medical oversight, and post-care program (3-12 months depending on program). Not included: transportation to/from the facility, pre-arrival labs and testing, and optional extended longevity programs.

We understand this is a significant investment in your wellbeing. Payment plans are available for qualified applicants. We also offer a limited number of scholarship positions for those who demonstrate genuine need and readiness for this work. Discuss options with your admissions advisor during your discovery call.

Integration is where transformation becomes lasting change. All programs include a structured post-care period (3-12 months) with coaching sessions, community access, practice guidance, and check-ins at key intervals. You'll also have lifetime access to our digital platform and alumni community. Many guests return for maintenance visits annually.

Signs of readiness include: a genuine desire for deep change (not just relief), willingness to do the inner work required, ability to take time for this process, openness to new perspectives, and some prior engagement with personal growth work. During your discovery call, we'll explore your readiness together and be honest about whether this is the right time.
