Guidance

How to Choose Your Program

The right program depends on your unique circumstances. During your consultation, we'll help you determine which journey best serves your transformation. Consider these factors:

Available Time

What window can you genuinely create for this work? Rushing transformation creates friction.

Depth of Work Needed

Complex trauma histories and major life restructuring benefit from longer containers.

Previous Experience

First-time guests may begin with shorter programs; those experienced may choose depth.

Support System

What integration support awaits you at home? Stronger support may allow shorter stays.
