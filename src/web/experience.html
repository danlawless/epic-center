The Experience

Seven Pillars of Transformation

A comprehensive approach that bridges cutting-edge medical science with sacred wisdom, luxury hospitality with deep healing, and technology with human connection.

Where Every Detail Serves Your Healing

Our methodology rests on seven foundational pillars, each essential to creating lasting transformation. These aren't separate programs - they weave together into a unified container designed to support you at every level: physical, neurological, emotional, and spiritual.

Pillar One

Medical & Scientific Oversight

Safety is sacred. Every system, protocol, and design decision prioritizes your wellbeing. Our evidence-based approach provides the foundation upon which all other pillars rest.

💬

Comprehensive Screening

Rigorous pre-arrival medical evaluation including cardiovascular screening, labs, and psychiatric assessment.

💓

Continuous Monitoring

24/7 cardiac monitoring, vital signs tracking, and medical staff presence throughout your stay.

📊

Measurable Outcomes

Validated clinical assessments at arrival and departure, with follow-up at 30, 90, and 365 days.

🚑

Emergency Preparedness

On-site physician, nursing staff, crash cart, and established hospital transfer protocols.

☤
[IMAGE: pillar-medical.jpg]

Pillar Two

Plant Medicine

The medicine is a catalyst, not the destination. We approach plant medicine with deep reverence for its origins, rigorous medical safety protocols, and a focus on integration that transforms experience into lasting change.

🌿

Sacred Container

Trauma-informed, ethically held ceremonies with 1:1 facilitator presence and strong medical oversight.

💜

Iboga & Ibogaine

Working primarily with these profound medicines known for nervous system reset and trauma resolution.

🌱

Ethical Sourcing

Sustainable cultivation, no wild-harvesting, and support for indigenous communities and conservation.

💡

Integration Focus

The ceremony is 10% of the transformation. The other 90% happens in the days, weeks, and months after.

🍃

Pillar Seven

Pre-Care & Post-Care

A retreat is not an event - it is a phase in a longer arc. We support the complete journey: preparation before arrival, immersion during your stay, and integration for months after you return home.

🕑

Pre-Care (4-8 Weeks)

🛠

Post-Care (3-12 Months)

Structured integration program, coaching sessions, community access, and practice maintenance support.

🏆

Life Restructuring

Support for identity anchoring, purpose alignment, and translating insights into lasting life changes.

🌐

Alumni for Life

Return visit options, continued bio-optimization access, and mentorship opportunities.
