Our methodology rests on seven foundational pillars, each essential to creating lasting change. Together, they form a comprehensive container for your healing journey.

01

Evidence-based protocols with rigorous safety screening, continuous monitoring, and measurable outcomes. Your safety is our sacred responsibility.

02

Plant Medicine

Trauma-informed, ethically held ceremonies with strong medical oversight. The medicine is a catalyst - the real transformation happens in integration.

03

The Complete Arc

Preparation. Experience. Integration.

A retreat is not an event - it is a phase in a longer arc of transformation. We support you before you arrive, during your stay, and long after you return home.

Phase 1

Awakening (Pre-Retreat)

4-8 weeks of medical screening, psychological preparation, and nervous system readiness practices.

Phase 2

Immersion (Retreat)

Your chosen program duration of deep healing, bio-optimization, ceremony, and integration.

Phase 3

Integration (Post-Retreat)

3-12 months of structured support, coaching, community connection, and practice maintenance.

Phase 4

Embodiment (Ongoing)

Lifetime alumni community, return visit options, and continued bio-optimization access.
